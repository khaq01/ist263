Kareem Haq's Biography

Kareem Haq was born on October 30, 2001 in Lahore, Pakistan. He grew up in Pakistan with his white mother and Pakistani father. Growing up he had identity troubles as he was biracial in a country that was primarily one race. He lived in Pakistan till he was 9 and moved because the country was in shambles.

Kareem had moved to the United States in the winter of 2010. At first it was difficult for him to assimilate to the culture but he gravitated to living in a more diverse country and made many friends of different cultures. Kareem attended Beall Elementary in Rockville, Maryland. There he would meet many people he's still close friends with to this day, In particular Jonathan and Gordon.

In the middle of 7th grade, Kareem had moved to a house in Potomac, Maryland. Until then, Kareem and his family lived in an appartment in Rockville, Maryland. Moving would mean Kareem would have to switch schools but he finished the rest of the school year at Julius West. Kareem's first class at his new school, Herbert Hoover middle school would be science with Mr. Braymayer. He was really anxious going into his first day but made friends with many people.
